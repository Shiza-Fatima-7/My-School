About Our School

Ibne Sina College is an English-medium school located in Lahore, Pakistan. It was established in 1987 and is well known for its strong academic standards.

Academic System

The school started with the Matric system and later moved to the Cambridge O-Level system. Teachers focus on understanding concepts instead of rote learning.

School Structure

The preschool section is co-educational. Primary and secondary sections are separate for boys and girls to maintain a disciplined learning environment.

Facilities

Our school provides:
• Science and computer labs
• A well-stocked library
• Playgrounds for sports
• Clean and comfortable classrooms

Character Building

Along with studies, students are taught discipline, good manners, and social responsibility. The school also arranges charity events and sports activities.
